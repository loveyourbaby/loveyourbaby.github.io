Breastfeeding With Certainty

Breastfeeding is spontaneous, natural, and happens anywhere moms are. We appreciate the right that all moms have to care for and feed their babies anywhere, any place, anytime. Each photo was taken in places both public and private and illustrate the nature and magnificence of motherhood.

You are invited to enter your pictures to the Love Your Baby Gallery. Please send your original photos.
